About DailyFX Delivery

Revolutionising parcel delivery across the United Kingdom with technology-driven logistics solutions.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, DailyFX Delivery emerged from a simple observation: parcel delivery in the UK needed a technological revolution. Our founders, with backgrounds in logistics and software engineering, set out to create a delivery service that puts transparency and reliability first.

Today, we serve thousands of customers daily, from individual senders to large e-commerce businesses. Our GPS-enabled tracking system provides real-time visibility into every shipment, while our network of professional drivers ensures your parcels arrive safely and on time.

We're not just a delivery company – we're a technology company that happens to deliver parcels.
