Delivery Questions

We deliver to all addresses across the United Kingdom, including England, Scotland, Wales, and Northern Ireland. For Highland, island, and remote areas, delivery may take an additional 1-2 business days. International shipping is available to select European destinations.

  • Same Day: 4-6 hours (order by 12pm for major cities)
  • Next Day: Delivered by 6pm the next business day
  • Standard: 2-3 business days

Our driver will attempt delivery and leave a card with instructions. You can choose to: have the parcel left in a safe place, delivered to a neighbour, redirected to a pickup point, or rescheduled for another day. You can update delivery preferences through our tracking page.

Standard parcel limits:

  • Maximum weight: 30kg per parcel
  • Maximum dimensions: 120cm x 60cm x 60cm
  • Maximum length + girth: 3 metres

For larger or heavier items, please contact us for a custom freight quote.

Tracking & Updates

Enter your tracking number (starting with DFX-) on our tracking page. You'll see real-time GPS location, estimated delivery time, and full delivery history. You can also track via our mobile app or by logging into your account dashboard.

  • Pending: Order received, awaiting collection
  • Collected: Picked up from sender
  • In Transit: On the way to destination depot
  • Out for Delivery: On the van for final delivery
  • Delivered: Successfully delivered

Yes, you can redirect your parcel before it's out for delivery. Log into your account, find the package, and click "Edit Delivery". Address changes may incur additional charges depending on the new location. Once the parcel is out for delivery, changes cannot be made.

Our GPS tracking updates every 30 seconds with accuracy within 10 meters. The map shows the driver's real-time location along with estimated arrival time. Note that in areas with poor signal, updates may be slightly delayed.
