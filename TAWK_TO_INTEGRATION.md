## 💡 Best Practices for Delivery/Tracking Site

### Suggested Greeting Messages:

**Online Greeting** (when you're available):
```
👋 Welcome to DailyFX Delivery!
Need help tracking your package or have delivery questions?
I'm here to assist you!
```

**Proactive Message** (after 30 seconds on tracking page):
```
🚚 Having trouble finding your package?
Type your tracking number and I'll help you locate it!
```

**Offline Message** (when unavailable):
```
Thanks for visiting DailyFX Delivery!
We're currently offline, but leave us a message and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.

For urgent tracking inquiries, visit our Track page.
```

### Quick Shortcuts to Create:

Add these in Tawk.to dashboard for faster responses:

1. **#track** - "Sure! Please provide your tracking number (e.g., DFX-12345678) and I'll check the status for you."

2. **#hours** - "Our customer service hours are Monday-Friday, 8am-8pm GMT. You can track packages 24/7 at https://consignment-site-2ac0cae70da0.herokuapp.com/track/"

3. **#delay** - "I apologize for the delay. Let me check your package status. Could you please provide your tracking number?"

4. **#delivered** - "Great! According to our records, your package was successfully delivered. Check the tracking page for delivery proof and signature."
